About Me

Just Because creations started out as a reminder from Jesus of something special my Father always said. We are worthy to be loved “Just Because” and We are worth joy “Just Because”. He would often leave this saying on love notes to my mother. As I prayed for a name for this project, the Lord brought me back to this reminding me that it’s all just because he loves me. This reminder brought me comfort as I grieved his passing earlier this year. As I get ready to create, I pray over each piece that the same love and joy that I experience would be passed along to each of you no matter the season and no matter the trial. You are worth it. I hope you enjoy seeing them as much as I enjoy creating them.
